Quick Guide

This is the official quick guide for the Silent Struggle RP Department of Justice.

  • All legal matters must be filed through a DOJ Ticket in Discord.
  • Clerks are available to assist with paperwork and fees during scheduled hours.
  • Court schedules are posted daily in #court-docket.
  • Evidence requires screenshots or video per SSRP Rule 34.0 — testimony alone is insufficient.
  • Value of Life (NVL): If outnumbered or outgunned, you must comply.
  • Roleplay Over Gunplay: Verbal initiation is required before hostile action.
  • Safe Zones: No criminal activity in hospitals, spawn areas, clothing stores, or police cells.
  • Scene Cooldown: Do not change clothing or store vehicles for 15 minutes after a scene.

Questions? Open a DOJ Ticket or ask in #welcome-info.
